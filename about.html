Welcome to Boston City

Welcome to Boston, the capital of Massachusetts and one of the oldest cities in the United States. Founded in 1630, Boston is steeped in history and played a pivotal role in America’s fight for independence. Known as the “Cradle of Liberty,” the city combines historic charm with modern innovation, making it a vibrant place to live, work, and visit

A city of history and Culture

Boston is home to some of the nation’s most significant historical landmarks. Walking along the Freedom Trail, visitors can explore 16 locations that played key roles in the American Revolution, including the Boston Common, Paul Revere’s House, and the Old North Church. In addition to its rich history, Boston has a thriving arts and cultural scene. The city boasts world-class museums like the Museum of Fine Arts, the Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum, and a variety of performing arts venues, including the Boston Symphony Orchestra and the Boston Ballet.
